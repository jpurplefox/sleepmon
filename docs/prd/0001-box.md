# Box

## Purpose

The **Box** is the **persistent** record of your team — the **source of truth** —
and, on top of that, a **panorama**: how much each Pokémon **produces** and which
**areas of the game** the team covers. It does not compare configurations — that's
[Comparison](0002-comparison.md) — it gives an **overview**: understand at a glance what you have and
where the gaps are.

It answers: *"which Pokémon do I have, how much does each one yield, and which
areas of the game (berries / ingredients / skills) do I have covered?"*.

**Box vs. Comparison.** Comparison puts configurations **side by side**, with a
base and deltas, to decide *which one yields more* (ephemeral). The Box is the
**real, complete** team, one entry per Pokémon, **with no base or deltas**: every
row reads on its own, and it persists.

## What it does (scope)

1. **Per-Pokémon overview** — one entry per Pokémon in the box, showing its
   identity, its configuration, and its **estimated production**.
2. **Sorting and filters** — to navigate the panorama as the box grows.
3. **Per-Pokémon actions** — Compare, Edit, and Delete.
4. **Box coverage** — which specialties, berries, and ingredients the team covers,
   and what is missing.

## How it works

### Per-Pokémon overview

Each Pokémon is an **entry** that groups, from left to right:

- **Identity**: sprite, name, ribbon (if any), and a gold **level badge**.
- **Configuration**: the equipped **ingredients**, the **nature**, and the **sub
  skills**, styled dimmer so they recede behind production.
- **Estimated production** (the central data), in a neutral color and at **equal
  hierarchy** across metrics (none is "the KPI"):
  - **Berries / day**.
  - **Strength / day**: the combined strength of berries + skill; when the skill
    contributes, the breakdown (berries + skill) is available on demand.
  - **Ingredients**: **all unlocked ingredients**, one per row (amount per day).
    When the Pokémon's skill produces ingredients, that output is **combined** per
    ingredient and marked with a skill indicator.
  - **Skill**: the main skill level, **triggers / day**, and, when the skill
    produces something, its **yield** (energy, random ingredients, dream shards,
    etc.).

Production arrives **already computed by the domain** (the same computation
Comparison uses); the Box **presents** it, it does not reimplement the formula.

### Sorting and filters

A **controls bar** above the list, shown only when there is **more than one
Pokémon** (with 0 or 1 there is nothing to sort or filter).

- **Sort** — a single control, with a direction toggle **↑/↓**. Default is
  **Pokédex ascending**, and **stable** (editing a Pokémon does not reorder it).
  Options: Pokédex number, level, berries/day, strength, ingredient production.
- **Filters** — **empty by default** (everything is shown). They combine with
  **AND across dimensions** and **OR within** a multi-select dimension:
  - **Type**: a grid of **berry icons** (in Pokémon Sleep type is 1:1 with berry,
    so one dimension covers both). Multi-select.
  - **Ingredient**: a grid of **ingredient icons**. Multi-select.
  - **Skill** (main skill): single-select, grouped by category; re-selecting the
    active value **clears it**.
  - **Specialty**: **Berries / Ingredients / Skills** toggles.
- **Active filters** show as **chips** with `×` to remove one, plus **"Clear"** to
  remove them all.

### Per-Pokémon actions

Each entry has an **overflow `···` menu** with, in order:

1. **Compare** — opens **Comparison** with that Pokémon as the base.
2. **Edit** — opens the [Pokémon form](0003-pokemon-form.md) to modify its
   configuration.
3. **Delete** — asks for **explicit confirmation** before removing.

### Box coverage

An aggregate aimed at *"what am I missing?"* (not frequencies), derived from the
box and the catalog, shown when there is **at least one Pokémon**:

- **Specialties**: a count per specialty (Berries / Ingredients / Skills). A
  missing specialty is shown as an **explicit 0** (absence is information).
- **Berry coverage** for the **Berry** specialists: a grid of **all** the game's
  berries; covered ones normal, missing ones **dimmed**, plus an **"X of Y"**
  count. A berry counts as covered if a Pokémon is a Berry specialist **or** if
  its skill contributes to the berry role (e.g. *Charge Strength*).
- **Ingredient coverage** for the **Ingredient** specialists: the same grid with
  **all** ingredients. Each Pokémon counts **only its main ingredient** (the one
  with the highest combined production, normal + skill).

## Acceptance criteria

- **Empty box** → an empty-state message ("The box is empty. Add your first
  Pokémon."), **not** an error.
- **Loading** → "Loading box…".
- **Load error** → "Couldn't load the box." with a **retry** option.
- With **0 or 1 Pokémon**, the **sort/filter bar does not appear**.
- The **default sort is Pokédex ascending**; editing a Pokémon **does not change
  its position**.
- **Combined filters**: selecting type *Fire* + ingredient *X* shows only Fire
  Pokémon **that also** have *X*; selecting two berries shows Pokémon with
  **either** of the two (OR within the dimension).
- **No filter matches** → "No Pokémon matches the filters." with a **"Clear"**
  button (distinct from the empty-box state).
- Each **active filter** shows as a **chip** with `×`; **"Clear"** removes them all
  and shows the whole box again.
- **Delete** always asks for confirmation ("Delete {species}?" · "This action
  can't be undone."); **cancelling deletes nothing**.
- **Compare** opens Comparison with that Pokémon as the base.
- **Coverage**: with no Berry specialists, the berry grid still appears with a
  count of **"0 of Y"** (it is not hidden); a specialty with no Pokémon shows
  **0**.
- **Per-Pokémon production** uses the **same domain computation** as Comparison:
  for the same configuration, the numbers match.

## Guidelines

- **Overview, not comparison.** No base, no deltas; every entry reads on its own.
  Comparing belongs to Comparison.
- **The computation lives in the domain.** The Box shows estimated production from the
  shared [Production model](0008-production-model.md); it does not invent or
  reimplement numbers.
- **Persistent source of truth.** What's in the Box persists; adding and editing go
  through the **Pokémon form**; destructive changes are confirmed.
- **Reuse the "Luz de luna" visual language.** The only gold in an entry is the
  **level badge**; metrics are neutral; the game's sprites and icons are the
  artwork; anything **not covered** is dimmed (same language as locked slots).
- **Closed specialties.** The curated catalog handles **Berries / Ingredients /
  Skills** (no "All-Rounder"); filters and coverage stick to that.
- **Always guide.** Explicit loading, error, and empty states.

## Out of scope

- **Comparing** configurations or hypothetical scenarios (that's Comparison).
- **Optimizing**: the Box does not suggest the "best" config or which Pokémon to
  add; it only shows the current panorama.
- **Frequency histograms** (counts of natures / sub skills / ingredients across the
  team): the Box's aggregate is **coverage**, not frequency.
